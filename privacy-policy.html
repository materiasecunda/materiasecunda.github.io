PRIVACY POLICY

Who We Are

Materia secunda (“us”, “we”, or “our”) is a creator, manufacturer, and retailer of fine fragrance products.

Personal Information We Collect and Why We Collect It

– Your full name, billing/mailing address, email address, telephone number, and applicable payment information are collected for the purposes of processing payments as required by our third-party platforms (such as Shopify Payments, Shop, PayPal, Stripe). We do not store your full credit card information.

– Most importantly, we do not share or sell any of our customers’ personal data.

Cookies

In general, https://materiasecunda.com uses cookies in order to improve the browsing experience. By utilizing cookies, users are able to add items to their shopping cart and have them be remembered the next time the website is visited, even if the current browsing session ends (e.g. by manually closing the browser). You as the user have the ability to enable or disable the creation and storage of cookies within your browser. Additionally, you as the user have the ability to delete cookies within your browser and/or on your system. Note that if you elect to disable cookie creation with respect to our website, that use of certain services on our website may be limited and in some cases, not possible.

Embedded Content From Other Websites

Embedded content refers to items such as videos, images, articles, etc. Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if you had visited the other website. These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracing your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website (e.g. an embedded YouTube video).

Data Retention

Any order placed on our website, all order information is retained unless you request data deletion.

Data Storage

The website https://materiasecunda.com, operated by Materia Secunda, uses Shopify and stores data on its servers. You can view Shopify's Privacy Policy here: https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy

Account Erasure Requests

If you wish to have your user account deleted, which includes personal data contained within any past order(s) received, please send your request via email to: info@materiasecunda.com. Note that within your request, you must provide the email address and username (if registered as a user) of your account, and that we will be sending a verification request to the supplied email address prior to fulfilling the account erasure request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: info@materiasecunda.com
